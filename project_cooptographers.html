    Game Design - Team Project - Tech Lead - Unreal Engine

    Co-optographers
    A local co-op multiplayer game where two players must work together to navigate through the depths of a forbidden temple, solving puzzles and clearing the path for the adventurers.

    Status: In progress (Jan 2022 - Ongoing)
    Engine: Created in Unreal Engine using Blueprints
    Team Size:   17 (Group)

    Features I worked on:
  • Co-op - Making it so the game can be played by two people.
  • Characters - setting up the characters with different abilities.
  • AI - the AI pathing.
  • Gamemode - the flow of the game.
