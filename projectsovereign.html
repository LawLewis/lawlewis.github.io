Sovereign

By Steven Lewis

What is it?

A top down roguelike RPG where the player ventures into caves to fight through enemies, gaining levels and equipment as they go until they reach the boss room. The goal of this project was to make a demo of a roguelike game where the player has different abilties, classes, equipment and stats. This was a group collab project and I worked as junior tech making things like movement, attacks, abilties, stats and UI.

Features

Abilities - Different abilities that are unlocked as you level up.
