Devolve Or Die

By Steven Lewis

What is it?

A top down space game where the player controls a space worm that must shrink to survive the collapsing universe. The game is built around the theme of "Devolution" and the goal is to shrink in size to be able to enter a wormhole and escape the collapsing universe. The devolution comes into play when the player has shrunk past certain milestones at which they must choose what abilities and bonuses they lose.

Features

Slither Movement - The player is always moving and controls the direction of the space worm.
Devolution - shrinking and devolving are the only way to reach the required size to escape.
Hunger - as the player shrinks their hunger will increase and they will lose control so maintaing a good level of hunger while continuing is the best way to win.
