About

    My name is Steven Lewis , I also go by Law Lewis online and I am based in the UK .

    Currently I'm in my third and final year of university studying Games Design and Programming at Staffordshire University in Stoke-on-Trent.

    I've been studying game development for 3 years now and the more I learn the more I get drawn in. As a lifelong gamer the sheer idea of being able to work on and create a game is outstanding. Games are an amazing way to express and experience creativity. I absolutely love working on games and love designing and scripting mechanics , making AI and coming up with some fun ideas .

    My dream is to get a fun and interesting job within the games industry , and I would love to get something that puts me on the right path to become a lead technical designer .

    I'm someone that loves to enjoy myself and I'm a big fan of games, movies, anime, manga and animals.

    Fun facts about me , before starting on my game dev journey I studied animal management, my favourite animal is the komodo dragon, I can drive a tractor and I've been skydiving.
