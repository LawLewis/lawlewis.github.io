What is it?

A third person RPG prototype where you play as a wizard completing guild quests to rise through the ranks, using a variety of spells and gaining experience as you go. DnD like stats that allows the player to choose what kind of adventure they want to have.

Features

Spell Book - Allowing different spells to be unlocked through levels and spells to be added to the hotbar.
Stats - A variety of stats that can be upgraded by levelling: health, mana, intelligence, wisdom, dexterity, strength.
Quests - Completable quests given by NPCs varying from fetch quests, escort quests and extermination quests.
